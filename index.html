AboutMe

What

Sonlet is a bookmarklet that lets you download audio from any supported youtube video directly as a mp3 file. Works only on desktop or places with bookmark bars !

How

  1. Drag this youtube mp3 to your bookmark bar.
  2. Go to any Youtube video (respect Youtube Terms) and press this link on the bar.
  3. Get the mp3 audio directly !
  4. Laugh, drink some beers, and do not forget oxford commas !
